Paper Submission

This section provides guidelines and access for paper submission to the NESS 2025 seminar.

Welcome to the Paper Submission Portal for the NESS 2025.

This page provides all the necessary information for submitting your research paper to be considered for presentation and publication in the NESS 2025 Seminar Proceedings. Authors of presentations delivered at the NESS 2025 Seminar are invited to submit their full papers through this portal. All submitted papers will undergo a peer-review process to ensure the quality and relevance of the contributions to the seminar theme.

Submission Deadline:
Submissions close on November 5, 2025, at 23:59 (local time). Late submissions will not be accepted.

Registration and Publication Payment:
Please note that accepted papers are subject to a payment covering both registration and publication fees. Payment details and instructions will be provided upon acceptance of your paper.

To submit your paper, please visit the NESS 2025 submission page on Morressier . If you do not yet have a Morressier account, please register before submitting your paper.
