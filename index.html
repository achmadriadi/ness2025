THE ANNUAL
NESS 2025 SEMINAR

“Advancing Eco-Friendly Marine Technologies through Risk Management: Norway-ASEAN Collaboration”

11-12 December 2025, Universitas Indonesia, Indonesia

About The Event

The Norway-ASEAN Consortium in Risk Management for Safer and Sustainable Ocean (NESS) holds its annual international seminar to address the balance between ocean resource utilization and environmental sustainability. NESS promotes risk management as a proactive tool to support safer and greener ocean practices. The consortium bridges Norway’s technical expertise with ASEAN’s practical experience and strong industry engagement. This platform fosters collaboration for sustainable solutions in ocean industries.
